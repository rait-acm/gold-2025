About Us

The Gathering of Outstanding Leaders in various domains (GOLD) event is a pioneering initiative designed to foster collaboration among ACM (Association for Computing Machinery)India chapters of Maharashtra region. This event will bring together leaders from diverse domains, including technology, research, and marketing, to deliver insightful talks. The event aims to inspire and empower attendees by showcasing innovative ideas and leadership strategies. A unique highlight of the GOLD event is the Reverse Coding(Website edition), a competitive segment where participants will showcase their creative and website building abilities. This event will serve as a platform for participants to exchange knowledge, enhance collaboration, and address real-world challenges through teamwork and innovation..

EVENT SCHEDULE



(8:00 AM - 9:30 AM):March 22

Registration

ACM chapter teams will register for the event at the registration desk. Teams will be provided with accreditations, including schedules and ideathon guidelines..

(10:00 AM - 10:30 AM)March 22

Inauguration

The event will be officially inaugurated with a welcome address by the organizing committee.

(11:00 AM - 1:00 PM):March 22

Speaker Talks

Renowned leaders from technology, research, and marketing domains will deliver 30-minute talks. Topics will include cutting-edge advancements, research methodologies, and market trends. A 10-minute Q&A session will follow each talk to engage the audience and address queries..

(1:00 PM - 2:00 PM):March 22

Lunch Break

A networking lunch will be served, encouraging informal discussions among participants, speakers, and organizers.

(2:00PM-5:00PM):March 22

"Reverse Coding" Website Edition

Participants will be shown a fully built website and challenge them to reverse-engineer the code as quickly as possible. Twist: They must complete the structure without viewing the source code—just by inspecting it visually.

(5:00 PM - 5:30 PM):March 22

Valedictory

The event will conclude with a valedictory ceremony, recognizing the efforts of all participants. Winners of the competition will be announced, and the winning ACM chapter will receive awards, including a trophy, certificates, and other accolades
